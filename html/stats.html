Probability

  • \(P(A) = u(A)\)
  • \(P(A \cup B)\), when \(A,B\) disjoint, is \(u(A)+u(B)\)

  • \(P(A|B) = \dfrac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)}\)

  • \(P(B|A) = \dfrac{P(A \cap B)}{P(A)} = \dfrac{P(A|B)*P(B)}{P(A)}\)
